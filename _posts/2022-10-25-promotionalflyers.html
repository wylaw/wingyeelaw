CLIENT

Rick and Susan Sontag Center for Creativity (the Hive)

PROJECT SUMMARY

A set of over 50 promotional flyers for workshops and events held at the Rick and Susan Sontag Center for Creativity. Each flyer incorporates my vector illustraions and is designed to reflect the type of activity done at the event or introduce guest workshop leaders and speakers. These flyers were used to advertise on multiple platforms including on the Hive website, Eventbrite, Instagram, and physically on the Claremont Colleges campuses with a different format to fit each platform. They aided in bringing in 350+ students to events over the course of a semester.
