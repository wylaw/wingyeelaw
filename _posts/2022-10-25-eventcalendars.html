CLIENT

Rick and Susan Sontag Center for Creativity (the Hive)

PROJECT SUMMARY

Semesterly calendars that detail the events held at the Rick and Susan Sontag Center for Creativity (the Hive). I formatted the page and text layout based on branding guides from the Hive. Over 500 are handed out at beginning of semester orientation events, club fairs, and in classes to advertise co-curricular offerings to students and community members of the Claremont Colleges. Additionally, they direct users to the Hive website for event sign ups, event planning for clubs and classes, and information on the curricular offerings at the Hive.
